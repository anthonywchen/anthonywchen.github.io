Anthony Chen

Welcome! I'm a fourth-year PhD student working on machine learning and natural language processing at UC Irvine where I have the pleasure of being advised by Sameer Singh.

My research is focused on the following topics:

Natural Language Generation: Generation is a fundamental task encompassing summarization, translation, and question answering, however, evaluation is challenging. We show that learned metrics (not engineered metrics) are a better path towards NLG evaluation for QA.

Long Tail of Natural Language: Natural distributions of language have a long tail which isn't well represented in existing datasets. I'm interested in designing datasets that can represent the long tail and creating models that are robust on these long tail instances.

Random Other Stuff: Whatever else I get distracted by.
