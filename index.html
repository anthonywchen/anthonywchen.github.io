Anthony Chen

Welcome! I'm an engineer at Google DeepMind. These days, I'm doing research on and excited by long-context models and their potential to subsume retrieval, RAG, and more.

I received my Ph.D. from UC Irvine advised by Sameer Singh. My dissertation focused on factuality and large language models with an emphasis on understanding and addressing the pernicious problem of hallucinations. During my doctorate I had the pleasure of doing research internships at Apple, Google, and Verneek.
