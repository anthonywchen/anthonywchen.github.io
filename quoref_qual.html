Read the instructions and examples, even if you have done similar tasks, as the examples may have changed.


Then answer the 10 questions, referring to the examples when necessary. You will need at least 8 correct to pass. You will be paid whether you pass or not.


If you pass, you will receive the "quoref" qualification. The qualification will allow you to work on the 1,000 HITs for this task that we will release over time.


In this task, you are given a passage, a question, a correct answer, and a predicted answer.

The task is to score how closely a predicted answer captures the same information (i.e. have the same meaning) as a correct answer using the passage, question, and human commonsense knowledge.

Scoring is on a scale from 1 to 5 .
A 1 score means the predicted answer captures none of the information of the correct answer, and thus is a completely wrong answer.
A 5 score means the predicted answer captures the same information as the correct answer, and thus is a completely right answer.


Note: A predicted answer may answer the question correctly, but to be given a score of 5, it must be equivalent to the correct answer.

Note: The score you give should not depend on the capitalization of the correct and predicted answer.

Note: A helpful tip for searching for the correct answer in the passage is using Control+F to search for it.

The couple subsequently relocated to Carnation, Washington and then to Seattle.Love's first major media exposure came in a September 1992 profile of herself and Cobain for Vanity Fair by journalist Lynn Hirschberg, entitled "Strange Love." After being asked to participate in a cover story for the magazine, Love was urged by her manager to accept the request. In the year prior, Love and Cobain had developed a heroin addiction, and the profile painted the couple in an unflattering light and suggested that Love had been addicted to heroin during her pregnancy. The article ultimately resulted in the Los Angeles Department of Children and Family Services investigating, and custody of Love and Cobain's newborn daughter, Frances, was temporarily awarded to Love's sister, Jaimee. Love claimed she was misquoted by Hirschberg, and asserted that she had immediately quit using heroin during her first trimester after she discovered she was pregnant. Love would later claim that the publication of the article had serious implications for her marriage as well as Cobain's mental state, suggesting it was a factor in his suicide.
